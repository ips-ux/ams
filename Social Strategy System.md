### **Identify**

*Create a content strategy which aligns with your goals.*

- Identify your **strengths** and **interests** you can apply to your content.
- Track **viral content** in parallel industries you can apply to music.
- Identify how you can adapt others’ content types to **your brand**.

### **Experiment**

*Begin creating a volume of content which aligns with your strategy.*

- **Prioritize your ideas** based on Value, Effort, and Alignment.
- Ideally pick a **variety of formats** and styles to find what works best.
- Create a **high volume** of ideas to increase chances of vitality.

### **Measure**

*Assess the performance results of your experiments.*

- Assess **performance results** of your content.
- Identify **patterns** among best-performing content.

### **Adapt**

*Update your strategy based on what’s working and what’s not.*

- **Apply learnings** from your previous Measurement step
- Continue experimenting while **implementing these learnings** with new content.

## 5. Content Framework

![Screenshot 2024-08-15 at 5.00.35 PM.png](https://prod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/bdb6ec4d-599f-4ea4-becd-d171452fb51a/bb3dc3c2-40c1-4c97-ad43-27786555ca35/Screenshot_2024-08-15_at_5.00.35_PM.png)

### **Hook Viewer**

Goal here is to **capture attention in the first few seconds**—this is critical on platforms where users scroll quickly through content.

- **Viral Hooks**: Find a viral video hook to immediately engage the viewer.
- **Movement**: Begin with bold gestures or dynamic camera movement to intrigue viewer.
- **Make Statement**: Ask a question or make a promise of what will occur in the video.
- **Trending Sounds**: Utilize trending audio in some way to hook the viewer.
- **Text Overlays**: Use text in your video to create curiosity around what is to come.

### Deliver Value

After hooking them, we need to deliver enough value to **make it worth their time to watch**. Value could be **entertainment**, **education**, or **inspiration**.

- **Entertainment**
    - Humor & comedy
    - Visual appeal
    - Climactic moment
    - Great music
- **Education**
    - Tutorials
    - Production tips
    - Behind scenes
    - Learnings
- **Inspiration**
    - Motivation
    - Compelling story
    - Announcements
    - Achievements

### Prompt Engagement

Goal is to **prompt some kind of engagement** to assist in the performance of our video.

- **Share**: Viewers will share videos that overdeliver in any of the 3 value categories.
- **Comment**: Ask viewers for their opinion, suggestions, or response to a video.
- **Save**: Viewers will save high value videos, but you can also prompt them to save in your video.
- **Follow**: By providing high value, they will likely follow you to experience more value in future.
- **Like**: Viewers will automatically like and favorite content they feel delivers significant value.

## 6. Platform Strategies

*Apply the strategies below to maximize your performance on each social platform listed.*

### **Instagram**

- **Overall**
    - **Branded**: Be sure that all your content aligns with the branding you have developed and envision for yourself.
    - **Congruent Aesthetic**:  Ensure you have consistent aesthetic, filtering, and coloring among your content.
    - **Aesthetic**: Ensure your profile maintains a consistent, professional, well-branded aesthetic at all times.
    - **Video-Focus**: 90% of your content should video, ideally reels or video posts. This will maximize your probability of discovery.
    - **Respond**: Ensure you respond to all comments or engagement on your content.
    - **Diversity**: Use a variety of content to keep your posts fresh and engaging, while not getting repetitive to you followers.
    - **Reciprocate**: Stay active on Instagram to engage with other artists, fans, or collaborators content and stories. Engagement is reciprocal.
    - **Pre-Loading**: Load your posts and all relevant descriptions ahead of time, making it seamless to post when you have you content scheduled to share.
- **Settings**
    - **Description**: Clear slogan or overview listed on profile, along with notable label releases or achievements.
    - **Contact**: Booking email linked with profile.
    - **Email**: Booking email clearly listed on profile.
    - **Username**: Social handle matches that on other platforms.
    - **Shortlink**: Your branded shortlink is linked in your bio.
    - **Handle**: Username aligns with that on other social platforms.
    - **Picture**: Well-branded profile picture assigned.
    - **Pinned Posts**: Strategically selected pinned posts show clear momentum.
    - **Story Highlights**: Highlights and branded thumbnails created.
    - **Maintenance**: All reels which doesn’t align with grid aesthetic have been removed from grid.

### **TikTok**

- **Overall**
    - **Frequency**: Post as frequently as possible, ideally 5 or more times per week.
    - **SEO**: Use indexible search terms in your content, captions, and description to optimize ranking when users search for specific terms (genre, artists, ect).
    - **Speak and Type**: Use your captions, post description, and your words to repeat your hook, value delivery, and engagement prompt.
    - **Batching**: Pre-load multiple draft posts in a single day, and use reminders in your AMS for when to post those drafts.
- **Strategy**
    - **Captions**: Use very descriptive and detailed captions to aid in post SEO. Ideally including criteria that would show up when someone searches related terms.
    - **Hashtags**: Use hashtags on every post, ranging from 1-3 very specific hashtags each.
    - **Invisible Tags**: Include keywords in your video’s captions that include terms a user may search for. Then pinch the screen to reduce the size of these tags, and move to the side to make them invisible to viewers.
    - **Editing**: Use the TikTok app itself for editing, as this tends to generate better performance.
    - **Thumbnails**: Use catchy thumbnails which will hook the viewer, while also being descriptive as to what content is to come.

### **YouTube**

- **Playlists**: Create a collection of playlist to categorize the different types of videos you upload. This could include: Music, Live Sets, Tutorials, Vlogs.
- **Featured Video**: Select a Featured video of your most successful post, or your most recent release.
- **Analytics**: Use tools like VidIQ or TubeBuddy to analyze channel performance, and assess the strategy and performance of related channels.
- **Tags**: Use TubeBuddy or VidIQ to assist with tag selection, and assess tag performance. Consider using tags which mention genre, similar artists, related labels, ect.
- **Algorithm Metrics**
    - **Watch Time**: The total amount of time viewers spend watching your videos is a critical metric.
    - **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares, and subscribes significantly impact your video's ranking.
    - **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: The ratio of clicks to impressions on your video thumbnails.
- **Profile**
    - **Channel** **Keywords**: Assign channel keywords to you account to aid in search discovery by new users.
    - **Playlists**: Create organization and structure on your channel using playlists. Suggested categories include music, live performances, tutorials, ect.
    - **Community**: Create your YouTube community to associate with your profile, and keep fans in this community frequently engaged.
    - **Biography**: Update your profile with your artist biography.
    - **Linked Profiles**: Link all associated social and streaming profiles with your account.
- **Growth Strategy**
    - **Optimize Metadata**: Use relevant and descriptive titles, tags, and descriptions.
    - **Thumbnails**: Design custom thumbnails that attract viewers and accurately represent the content of your videos.
    - **Engage**: Respond to comments and build a loyal community.
    - **Be Consistent**: Upload videos on a regular basis
    - **Playlists**: Create playlists for viewers to watch multiple videos in one session, increasing watch time.
    - **Curators**: Send your new music to playlist curators to share with their audience
    - **YouTube Analytics**: Use YouTube Analytics to track performance and understand your audience’s behavior, preferences, and demographics.
    - **Consistency**: Regular uploads and consistent content help keep your channel active and maintain viewer interest.
    - **Thumbnails**: Design custom thumbnails that attract viewers and accurately represent the content of your videos.
    - **Engage**: Respond to comments and build a loyal community.
    - **Be Consistent**: Upload videos on a regular basis
    - **Curators**: Send your new music to playlist curators to share with their audience
    - **YouTube Analytics**: Use YouTube Analytics to track performance and understand your audience’s behavior, preferences, and demographics.

### **Facebook**

- **Personal Page**
    - **Frequency**: Share all important updates regarding your project on your personal page.
    - **Name**: Include your artist name in your full name on your profile.
    - **Bio**: Contains overview of your artist project and a fan link.
    - **Friends**: Consistently adding and accepting friends to reach 5k total friends.
    - **Picture**: Select a profile picture which makes it clear you are an upcoming artist.
    - **Content**: Share all important announcements from you artist page to your personal page as well as separate posts.
- **Artist Page**
    - **Invite**: Take the time to invite every friend to like your page.
    - **Frequency**: We recommend at least once per week.
    - **Cross-Post**: Share all your TikToks and IG reels on Facebook as well.
    - **Description**: Include a brief artist bio, as well as your booking contact information.
    - **Linked Accounts**: Include links to all other relevant social and streaming platforms.
    - **Picture**: Use a well-branded profile picture which aligns with that on other platforms.
    - **Banner**: Update your banner regularly with each release to gain additional reach on each release.
- **Fan Group**
    - **Give Value**: Offer incentives and give value to those in your community, such as guest list spots, early listens on music, ect.
    - **Incentivize**: Provide an incentive for people to join your group, such as early downloads on music or first access to merch.
    - **Ask**: Create posts asking fans questions or their opinions about music, recent events, ect.
    - **Converse**: Use your community as a more intimate setting to engage with your fans, get their opinions, and allow them to provide input on your project.
    - **Moderate**: Ensure you or someone on your team moderates the posts and potential spam in your community.
    - **Banner**: Update your banner regularly with each release to gain additional reach on each release.
    - **Welcome**: Create posts to welcome new members and ask them to share something about themselves every other week or so.

### **X / Twitter**

- **Overall**
    - **Frequency**: We recommend at least 7 posts per week.
    - **Competitive Analysis**: Study the strategies and types of content used by similar artists or pages in your industry.
    - **Cross-Posting**: Post your most successful tweets to your other pages as a screenshot, or video further elaborating on them.
- **Settings**
    - **Picture**: Use a well-branded profile picture which aligns with that on other platforms.
    - **Contact**: Make your booking contact information clearly visible.
    - **Banner**: Select a banner image which portrays clear momentum for your project.

    4. Tap **“Use Template”**.
    5. Upload your clips/photos when prompted.
    6. Export your video or save as a draft.

    - Ensure you’re logged into the **same CapCut account** on both mobile and desktop.
